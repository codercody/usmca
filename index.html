Who we are

The US Math Comptetition Association is a group of math competitions with the goal of working together to bring the best math contest experience to all competitors.

Our goals

Organize Students

  • A contest calendar and map so you never miss events near you.

  • Competition profiles for getting the information you need.

  • One account for registration in all competitions.

  • Result tracking across all different contests you participated in.

Assist Contests

  • Resources for forming and organizing competitions.

  • A shared problem database so unselected problems don't go to waste.

  • A network of organizers, test solvers, and volunteers.

  • A review system to help competitions improve.

Connect Sponsors

  • A centralized location for sponsors to identify promising events.

  • A team dedicated to sponsor relations.
